Our Brand Identity

Floral Boutique is positioned as the premier luxury floral studio of DHA Rawalpindi — a brand that breathes feminine elegance, editorial beauty, and emotional storytelling. We are inspired by the world's most coveted lifestyle brands: the refined minimalism of Aesop, the romantic heritage of Dior, the sophisticated aesthetic of Parisian gifting studios and Korean floral ateliers.

Our ecommerce vision transforms gifting into a seamless, beautiful digital experience — allowing customers across Rawalpindi, Islamabad, and beyond to order luxury floral arrangements through Instagram, our website, and WhatsApp with the same elegance they would expect in-store.

Ivory & Champagne — The Foundation
Our background tones evoke purity, elegance, and understated wealth. Ivory communicates timelessness and refinement — it is the colour of fine linen, Dior packaging, and the pages of a luxury magazine. Used as the dominant background, it creates breathing room that makes every floral element sing.
Blush & Dusty Rose — The Heart
These feminine, romantic tones are the emotional core of Floral Boutique. Blush rose communicates warmth, love, and gentle luxury without the aggression of bright pink. It is the colour of fresh peonies, satin ribbons, and the soft light of a Parisian afternoon — deeply aspirational and utterly feminine.
Champagne Gold — The Signature
Used sparingly as an accent, champagne gold communicates premium quality, celebration, and aspirational luxury. It appears on logo marks, price points, dividers, and packaging details — always as a signal of excellence. Never brash, always refined.
Deep Espresso — The Anchor
This rich, warm dark tone provides depth and sophistication without the coldness of pure black. Used for packaging backgrounds, dark-mode social content, and typographic anchoring, it creates a sense of editorial luxury that elevates everything placed against it.
